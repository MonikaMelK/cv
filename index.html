MeldiDesign

Engineer Architect based in Warsaw, Poland

I design buildings and interiors. I am mainly focused on ecological solutions. In my work, I try to look at the problem broadly and solve it holistically. I am a great team player, and I simply love working with specialists from other fields. I'm a geek when it comes to using new software, programming is a big part of a design process, starting from the very beginning, where environmental analysis is a core for future concepts. My education is at the verge of classical architectural education and self-teaching by using Internet sources and courses, and many wonderful books on a variety of subjects. The great thing about studying at a private school was the experience of freedom, the feeling I could push my ideas through, listening carefully to what the instructor had to say, and yet not being too influenced and lost. I am truly thankful I had such an opportunity to not be closed or limited by the instructor's imagination.
